Demand and Supply

Multiple Choice
Identify the choice that best completes the statement or answers the question.
 

 1.

b.
a decrease in the demand for gasoline.
c.
no change in the quantity of gasoline demanded.
d.
a decrease in the quantity of gasoline demanded.
e.
More information is needed to determine if the demand for gasoline increases or decreases.
 

 2.

b.
quantity demanded of Barbie dolls when the price is $12 per doll.
c.

d.

e.
Both answers A and C are correct.
